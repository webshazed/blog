Detailed Reviews

1. Ninja BN701 Professional Plus Blender

The Ninja BN701 is our top pick for consistent, bar-quality margaritas. Its 1400 peak watts of power and specialized Auto-IQ program for frozen drinks make crushing ice into a fine, slushy texture effortless. The 72-oz. Total Crushing® pitcher is built for durability, with a sleek dark grey design that looks good on any countertop.

Pros:
  • Unmatched ice-crushing power for perfectly smooth margaritas.
  • Dedicated "Frozen Drink" Auto-IQ program takes the guesswork out of blending.
  • Large 72-oz BPA-free pitcher is ideal for hosting.
  • Excellent build quality and very few reported durability issues.
Cons:
  • Louder than some competitors during operation.
  • The pitcher and lid are not dishwasher-safe (hand wash recommended).

2. Ninja BL660 Professional Compact Smoothie & Food Processing Blender

Almost as powerful as its BN701 sibling, the BL660 offers incredible value. Its 1100-watt motor and three functions (Frozen Drinks, Smoothies, Sauces) handle ice with ease. The kit includes two 16-oz. to-go cups with spout lids, making it perfect for preparing individual margaritas to enjoy on the patio. It's a versatile workhorse that won't break the bank.

Pros:
  • Excellent price-to-performance ratio for frozen drinks.
  • Comes with convenient single-serve cups for personal batches.
  • "Frozen Drink" function is highly effective for margaritas.
  • Compact design saves counter space.
Cons:
  • Slightly less raw power than the 1400W Ninja models for the largest ice loads.
  • The to-go cup seals can occasionally be tricky to lock perfectly.
