Buying Guide: How to Choose the Right Dolphin Pool Cleaner

Selecting the ideal Dolphin pool cleaner requires matching the model’s capabilities to your pool’s specific needs. Consider these key factors:

  • Pool Size and Type: Above-ground pools typically require cleaners with gentler navigation and liner-friendly brushes (e.g., E10). In-ground pools, especially larger ones (over 40 ft), benefit from models with enhanced climbing and capacity (e.g., Premier, CC Supreme). Always check the maximum pool length rating.
  • Filtration System: Top-load filter access (Nautilus CC, E10, CC Supreme, Escape) significantly simplifies maintenance. Consider ultrafine filters if you have fine sand or silt, or oversized leaf bags for areas with heavy foliage.
  • Navigation and Scrubbing: Basic random navigation is sufficient for simple pool shapes. For complex layouts or waterline stains, prioritize models with advanced scrubbers (Proteus DX3, CC Supreme) and strong track systems (Escape).
  • Smart Features: Wi-Fi and app control (CC Supreme) offer convenience for scheduling and manual operation, but assess whether the premium is justified given potential software hiccups.
  • Warranty and Support: Dolphin typically offers 1-3 year warranties. Longer coverage can be a sign of manufacturer confidence, especially for higher-end models.
  • Pool Surface Material: Vinyl-lined above-ground pools require gentler brushes; concrete or fiberglass in-ground pools can handle more aggressive scrubbing.
